Bengals beat Seahawks in overtime on Mike Nugent field-goal

January 3rd 2003

In 2015 the Bengals were undefeated going into week five undefeated. However, they were up against the Seattle Seahawks, who had just made a trip to the superbowl the previous season. The Bengals ended up winning with an overtime field goal and went on to win 12 games that season.
