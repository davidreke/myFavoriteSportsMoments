2003 National Championship Overtime Pass Interference Call

January 3rd, 2003

In 2003 this failed pass at the end of the first overtime would have ended the game and given the Hurricanes the national championship. However a late flag overturned the result of the play. Ohio state went on to win the game in double overtime.
